About Us

Founded in 2010, but with over 10 years experience in the industry, WP Electrical Services are a successful and expanding NICEIC certified company that deal with a large range of contracts ranging from simple domestic installations to complex production line control systems.

In addition to our installation services we also offer maintenance for existing systems. Currently we have a maintenance contract with the Longleat Group which we have been cooperating with for over 2 years successfully.

While based in Nailsea, Bristol, we cover the entire South West and offer a friendly service for all jobs both big and small. All contracts are self-certified and we pride ourselves on customer satisfaction.

We always offer a clear breakdown of labour and material costs on our invoices and offer no call out charge. In addition we will always leave the location clean and tidy after completion of the contract for your convenience.

We are also able to offer category 5 or 6 structured computer networking systems and fibre optics installation and maintenance services.

Absolutely all of our electricians are fully qualified with 17th edition wiring regulations and some of our certifications and qualifications are listed below:

  • Level 2 Certificate in Fundamental Inspection, Testing and Initial Verification
  • Level 3 Certificate in the Requirements for Electrical Installations (16th to 17th edition update to BS 7671 2008)
  • Level 2 Certificate in Electrotechnical Technology - Installation (Buildings And Structures)
  • Level 3 Certificate in Electrotechnical Technology - Installation (Buildings And Structures)
  • Electrotechnical Advanced Apprenticeship Buildings And Structures

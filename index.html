Welcome

Based in Nailsea, Bristol; WP Electrical Services provide friendly, high quality and cost effective electrical solutions for Nailsea and the surrounding Bristol area.

Our electricians are fully NICEIC certified and have 10 years of professional experience working in a range of situations involving domestic, industrial and commercial projects.
